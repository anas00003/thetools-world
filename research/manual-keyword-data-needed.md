# Manual Keyword Data Needed

Collect this data manually and feed it back before building new pages.

## Seed Keywords

- image compressor
- image resizer
- compress image under 100kb
- compress image under 200kb
- compress jpg under 100kb
- resize image for online form
- resize signature to 20kb
- passport photo resizer
- convert webp to jpg
- png to jpg
- jpg to png
- bulk image compressor
- pdf compressor
- jpg to pdf
- word counter
- age calculator
- qr code generator
- json formatter

## What To Collect For Each Keyword

- Google Keyword Planner screenshots or CSV export.
- Average monthly searches.
- Competition level.
- Top of page bid range if available.
- Google Trends comparison screenshot.
- Regions/countries with strongest interest.
- Top 10 Google result URLs.
- People Also Ask questions.
- Autocomplete suggestions.
- Notes on whether results are tools, guides, marketplaces, or mixed.

## Search Console Data After Launch

After `thetools.world` is live and verified, export:

- Queries.
- Pages.
- Clicks.
- Impressions.
- CTR.
- Average position.
- Date range.
- Country/device filters if useful.

## How To Feed Data Back

Use one batch per keyword cluster. Include the keyword, volume screenshot/export, Trends screenshot, top 10 URLs, and any notes about intent. The next build prompt should use that data to decide whether to build, merge, delay, or avoid.
