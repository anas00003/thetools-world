# TheTools.World AdSense Readiness Checklist

Do not add AdSense code until the site is live, stable, and ready for review.

## Required Trust Pages

- About page explains the site honestly.
- Contact page includes a working contact email.
- Privacy Policy mentions cookies, analytics, ads, and third-party services generally.
- Terms page is readable and accessible.
- Sitemap page is visible.

## Navigation And Content

- Navigation is clear on desktop and mobile.
- Footer links to About, Contact, Privacy Policy, Terms, and Sitemap.
- Every indexable page has useful original content.
- Tool pages include instructions and related tools.
- No copied content.
- No empty pages.
- No indexable coming-soon pages.
- No thin doorway pages.

## Ad Policy Safety

- No misleading download buttons.
- No fake ad placeholders.
- No ad-click encouragement.
- No prohibited content.
- No fake testimonials, fake user counts, or unverifiable claims.
- No keyword stuffing.

## Mobile And UX

- Upload controls are usable on mobile.
- Text and controls do not overlap.
- Pages load quickly.
- Error states are understandable.
- Ads, when later added, must not hide core tool controls.

## When To Apply

Apply after:

- `thetools.world` is connected and live with HTTPS.
- All clean URLs and redirects are verified.
- Sitemap is submitted in Search Console.
- Core legal pages are live.
- At least the existing image tools work reliably.
- There is no old-brand or placeholder residue.

## What Not To Do Before Approval

- Do not add empty ad boxes.
- Do not insert placeholder ad copy.
- Do not publish fake future tools.
- Do not mass-create low-value pages.
- Do not use aggressive popups or misleading buttons.
- Do not apply while DNS, SSL, or routing is unstable.
