# AI and GEO Readiness Checklist

Status: crawlability and content-clarity checklist for normal users, search engines, and AI search systems. This is not an instruction to add AI-only content.

## Current crawl readiness

- Canonical domain: `https://thetools.world`
- Sitemap: `https://thetools.world/sitemap.xml`
- Robots file allows normal crawling and now explicitly allows major search and AI crawlers.
- Assets for logo, favicon, and manifest are not blocked.
- No `llms.txt` is added.
- No unsupported AI-specific metadata is added.

## Page-level readiness signals

Important tool pages should include:

- A clear direct answer near the top.
- What the tool does.
- Who it is for.
- How to use it.
- Limitations or troubleshooting.
- Supported inputs and formats.
- Browser-side privacy note.
- Related tools.
- FAQ where useful.
- No AI-gimmick language.
- No hidden, cloaked, or bot-only content.

## Current assessment

Strong GEO readiness:

- Exact-size image and JPG compression pages.
- Form/photo preparation pages.
- Homepage and main tool hubs.

Acceptable GEO readiness:

- `/convert-webp-to-jpg`
- `/jpg-to-png`
- `/png-to-jpg`
- `/contact`
- Legal and sitemap pages.

Watchlist:

- Format converter pages should later receive more visible format guidance, troubleshooting, and FAQ content.
- `/contact` can remain simple, but should stay useful and transparent.

## Guardrails

- Do not add hidden text for AI systems.
- Do not keyword stuff.
- Do not invent authority claims.
- Do not add unsupported AI crawler files.
- Keep improvements useful for human visitors first.
